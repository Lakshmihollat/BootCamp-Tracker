Week 2: Titanic Survival Analysis (Mini-Project)

Explore the Titanic dataset to identify patterns and factors related to passenger survival using data cleaning, visualization, and EDA techniques.

Objective: Perform EDA to find patterns in survival based on features like gender, class, age, family aboard, and embarkation point. Use .groupby(), value_counts(), and charts (bar plots, histograms, heatmaps).

Steps:

  1. Load and inspect the data
  2. Preprocess the data
  3. Visualize the data: Example plots ‚Äî survival vs. gender, class distribution, etc.
  4. Perform EDA: Identify key trends and correlations. Investigate how variables like age, class, fare, and gender influence survival.

Predict & Analyze:

  • Basic Survival Stats (What % survived overall; Did more men or women survive?)
  • Survival by class
  • Survival by age
  • Survival by family aboard (SibSp + Parch)
  • Survival by port of embarkation
  • Were passengers with higher fares more likely to survive?
  • Which age group (child, adult, elderly) had better chances?
